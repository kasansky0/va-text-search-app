ORDER

Entitlement to service connection for emphysema is granted.

FINDING OF FACT

In May 2024, the rating office (RO) granted service connection for emphysema, which is binding on the Board.

CONCLUSION OF LAW

The criteria for entitlement to service connection for emphysema have been met. 38 C.F.R. 3.303.

REASONS AND BASES FOR FINDING AND CONCLUSION

The present appeal includes a claim for service connection for emphysema. In May 2024, the RO separately granted service connection for emphysema. This constitutes a favorable finding and is binding on the Board. For purposes of the present appeal, therefore, service connection for emphysema is granted. The claim is fully resolved in the Veteran's favor.
