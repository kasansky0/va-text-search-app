ORDER

Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of flexion associated with the service-connected right knee disability is dismissed.

Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of extension associated with the service-connected right knee disability is dismissed.

Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for meniscal impairment associated with the service-connected right knee disability is dismissed.

Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for scar residuals associated with the service-connected right knee disability is dismissed.

Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for a right ankle disability is dismissed.

FINDING OF FACT

1. In a VA Form 21-4138 (Statement in Support of Claim) received on February 24, 2025, the Veteran withdrew his Notice of Disagreement dated December 23, 2024. 

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

1. The criteria for withdrawal of the appeal of entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of flexion associated with the service-connected right knee disability are met. 38 U.S.C. § 7105; 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

2. The criteria for withdrawal of the appeal of entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of extension associated with the service-connected right knee disability are met. 38 U.S.C. § 7105; 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

3. The criteria for withdrawal of the appeal of entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for meniscal impairment associated with the service-connected right knee disability are met. 38 U.S.C. § 7105; 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

4. The criteria for withdrawal of the appeal of entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for scar residuals associated with the service-connected right knee disability are met. 38 U.S.C. § 7105; 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

5. The criteria for withdrawal of the appeal of entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for a right ankle disability are met. 38 U.S.C. § 7105; 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

REASONS AND BASES FOR FINDING AND CONCLUSIONS

The appellant is a veteran (the Veteran) who had active-duty service from September 1999 to November 2006. 

This appeal comes before the Board of Veterans' Appeals (Board) from a May 2023 rating decision of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), which was subject to a Higher Level Review decision dated December 20, 2024.

WITHDRAWAL OF APPEAL

1. Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of flexion associated with the service-connected right knee disability.

2. Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for limitation of extension associated with the service-connected right knee disability.

3. Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for meniscal impairment associated with the service-connected right knee disability.

4. Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for scar residuals associated with the service-connected right knee disability.

5. Entitlement to an effective date earlier than April 17, 2023, for the establishment of service connection for a right ankle disability.

The Board may dismiss any appeal which fails to allege specific error of fact or law in the determination being appealed. 38 U.S.C. § 7105. 

An appeal may be withdrawn as to any or all issues involved in the appeal at any time before the Board promulgates a decision. 38 C.F.R. § 20.205. 

Withdrawal may be made by the appellant or by his or her authorized representative. 38 C.F.R. § 20.205.

Here, the Veteran initiated the current appeal by Notice of Disagreement received on December 23, 2024, specifying the matters listed above. On February 24, 2025, the Veteran submitted a VA Form 21-4138 (Statement in Support of Claim). On that form, the Veteran withdrew in writing the "NOD on the date of 12/23/2024." This constitutes a withdrawal of the appeal in its entirety. Accordingly, the appeal  is dismissed.
